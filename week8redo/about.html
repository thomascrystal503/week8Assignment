Get to Know Us

We re enthusastic gardeners of both vegetables and flowers. Our enthusiasm, however, usually is rewarded with an untidy, unsymmetrical mess by harvest time. Our monthly newsletter provides information about sustainable techniques that will increase yield whether a garden is weeded or not.

The most natural gardening is permaculture. The permaculture garden mimics nature and encourages native plants and wildlife to flourish. The lazy 'permie' will appreciate the low-maintenance, food-producing garden for many years.

The fun in gardening is not in weeding or watering daily. The delight comes from the daily miracle of observation.
